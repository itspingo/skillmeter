Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our platform.

What makes your AI question generation unique?

Our AI analyzes the latest industry trends and job requirements to generate dynamic, relevant, and unique questions that accurately assess a candidate's skills, going beyond static question banks.

How does the anti-cheating technology work?

We use a combination of screen monitoring, webcam proctoring, and behavioral analysis to detect suspicious activity. Our system flags things like multiple browser tabs, face detection, and unusual keystroke patterns.

Can I customize my test questions?

Yes, you can manually add your own questions, edit AI-generated ones, and combine them to create a test that perfectly aligns with your specific needs.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. We also offer invoicing for our Enterprise plan.
